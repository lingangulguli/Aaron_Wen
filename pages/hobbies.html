How Gaming Informs My Work

Every game I play is also a case study. I analyze engagement mechanics, monetization strategies, update cadences, and community management. This deep familiarity with player expectations helps me build better products and provide more actionable strategic insights.

At Sega, my live-ops model drew directly from patterns I'd observed across hundreds of game updates. At Tencent, my understanding of what makes pre-registration campaigns viral came from years of participating in them as a player.
